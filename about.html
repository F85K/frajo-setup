About us

フラジョミダ

Frajomida is a small, hands-on team building a secure, multi-tenant hosting platform. We give administrators full control while keeping every client isolated and safe.

What we care about

  • Security-first: per-tenant databases, scan reports before deploy, and least-privilege access.
  • Simplicity: one clear API to provision, scan, and deploy.
  • Transparency: clients see their own status, logs, and reports—nothing more.
  • Reliability: automated checks and repeatable deployments backed by containers.

The team

We’re students and builders combining security practices with a practical developer experience. Our goal: make deploying secure apps as straightforward as clicking “Deploy”.
